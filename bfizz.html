Brewed Bold

Crafted for the fearless, BFizz redefines boldness by virtue of its unique taste – a robust malt flavoured drink with a strong sip of fizz that is sure to add bass to your tone the moment you down it. Enjoy the crisp concoction of fermented malt flavour, bitter hop notes along with the sweet hint of apple flavour, all infused into one rugged drink that’ll charge you right up to seize the day. BFizz commands attention without trying too hard with its striking white and red packaging. It is available in PET bottles of 125ml, 250ml and 600ml & also in 250ml cans.
